Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

This is one of my favorites my mom would make every week. This recipe uses a crockpot so it is easy to make and with a small amount of dishes to clean. Perfect for a night where you don't have much time to cook.

Ingredients

  • Skinless Chicken Breast
  • Cream of Chicken
  • Egg Noodles
  • Chicken Boullion

Steps

  1. In crockpot, add chicken brease and cream of chicken and some chicken boullion
  2. Mix together into a homogenous mixture.
  3. Set time on medium and cook for 8 hours
  4. With one hour remaining (after 7 hours) take out the chicken and shred into pieces
  5. Put the shredded chicken back in and add the egg noodles
  6. After an hour, you have your chicken noodle soup ready to eat!
